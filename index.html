About This Device

🤖

Galaxy AI — Built-In Intelligence

The S25 Ultra integrates Galaxy AI deeply into daily tasks. Circle to Search lets you search anything on screen, Live Translate breaks language barriers in real time, and ProScaler uses AI to upscale images with stunning clarity. Generative Edit empowers you to reshape photos with a single tap.

📸

ProVisual Engine — Redefine Photography

The industry-leading 200MP main sensor with multi-directional PDAF captures every detail in breathtaking clarity. Paired with a 5x optical telephoto and 120° ultra-wide, you get a complete photographic system. 8K video recording at 30fps means your memories are cinema-quality.

🔩

Grade 5 Titanium — Built to Last

Forged from aerospace-grade Titanium, the Galaxy S25 Ultra is as durable as it is premium. IP68 water and dust resistance means it handles life's unpredictable moments, while Gorilla Armor 2 dramatically reduces reflections for a clearer display in any light.

✏️

S-Pen — Your Ideas, Unleashed

The precision-engineered S-Pen is built right into the S25 Ultra's chassis, always ready when inspiration strikes. Sketch, annotate, sign documents, and navigate the UI with unmatched accuracy. A true productivity powerhouse, always at hand.
